Cloud Load Control helps enterprises to manage their containerized applications in large-scale IT environments.

Container frameworks, such as Docker, have proven to be an effective instrument to package, ship and execute applications in isolation from each other. Container technology drives workload mobility, scalability and the use of modern, flexible system design principles, such as microservices. In enterprise environments, the adoption of this technology often leads to a significant number of containerized workloads where the lifecycle needs to be controlled.

Easy Workload Management

Cloud Load Control helps enterprises to manage their containerized workload. It seamlessly integrates with existing OpenStack environments and provides a high degree of automation to address the constantly increasing amount and complexity of workload in enterprises.

Fast Container Deployment

Cloud Load Control enables the fast deployment of Docker containers on multiple servers, where it constantly monitors the workload state and controls its replication.

Kubernetes Enhancement

Cloud Load Control enhances Kubernetes, a technology which incorporates the experiences of over ten years of running containers workload at scale, for enterprise usage on OpenStack with proven Fujitsu quality.
